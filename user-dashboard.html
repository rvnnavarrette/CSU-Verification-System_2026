Help & FAQ
Find answers to common questions about the CSU Document Verification System. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to the Registrar's Office directly.

Verification typically takes 3–5 business days after submission. Processing time may vary depending on the volume of requests and completeness of your submitted documents. Your dashboard status updates in real time — no need to email or call to check.

Graduates — Transcript of Records (TOR), Diploma, or Certificate of Grades.

Undergraduates — Certificate of Grades or Enrollment Certificate.

Document upload is optional but speeds up the process. Files must be clear and legible. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG (max 10 MB each).

Yes — you can cancel a request as long as its status is still Pending. Go to My Requests, click View on the request, then click Cancel Request in the modal footer. Once a request has been reviewed (Verified or Not Verified) it can no longer be cancelled.

Your Overview dashboard and My Requests table both update automatically. The status will change from Pending to Verified or Not Verified once the Registrar's Office has reviewed your submission. Click View on the request to see the admin's remarks and download your verification certificate.

The system accepts PDF, JPG / JPEG, and PNG files. Each file must be 10 MB or less. You can upload multiple files per request. For best results, scan documents at a minimum of 150 DPI and make sure all text is clearly readable before uploading.
